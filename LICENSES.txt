HOEPS2
======
A PS2 homebrew game. Source game jam project "Hell on Earth" (2020), ported to PlayStation 2.
Copyright (c) 2026 2A Games

THIRD-PARTY COMPONENTS
-----------------------

Tyra - Game Engine for PlayStation 2
  https://github.com/h4570/tyra
  Copyright (c) Sandro Sobczynski and contributors
  Licensed under the Apache License, Version 2.0
  https://www.apache.org/licenses/LICENSE-2.0
  Modifications made to Tyra source:
    - engine/src/audio/audio_song.cpp: replaced fopen/fread/fclose/fseek
      with POSIX open/read/close/lseek to eliminate heap fragmentation leak
      during WAV streaming.
    - engine/src/audio/audio_adpcm.cpp: added playPooled() method for
      round-robin SFX channel allocation with steal-oldest fallback.
    - engine/src/renderer/core/texture/renderer_core_texture_sender.cpp/.hpp:
      added 128-slot texbuffer_t pool to avoid per-frame heap allocation.
    - engine/src/memory/frame_arena.cpp/.hpp: added 64KB bump-pointer arena
      (g_frameArena) reset each frame via RendererCore::endFrame().
    - engine/src/renderer/3d/mesh/mesh_material.cpp: MeshMaterial copy
      constructor now inherits ambient from source material (was previously
      hardcoded to 128,128,128,128).

ps2sdk - Homebrew PlayStation 2 SDK
  https://github.com/ps2dev/ps2sdk
  Copyright (c) ps2dev contributors
  Licensed under the Academic Free License, Version 2.0

tinyobjloader - .obj/.mtl parser (bundled with Tyra)
  https://github.com/tinyobjloader/tinyobjloader
  Copyright (c) Syoyo Fujita and contributors
  Licensed under the MIT License
